Overview

Purpose

[1. Idea/Goal a. Personal Music-Business Webpage. Make it easy for listeners to find information about me and my music all in one place. b. (Those who fulfill a certain requirement will get a free something or deal) 2. Site Explanation (Purpose and Function) a. I want my website to invite people to listen to my music, seek it out, and share it. I want it to not only show people where to find my music, but to tell the story behind each song and get to know me in a personal way through such to establish a personal connection with each listener. 3. USP (Unique Selling Proposition) a. Websites make it easy to find a plethora of information in one place. My site will allow listeners to find pertinent information about the artist in a simple and efficient way and make it easier for others to find the same as well. 4. Engage with a Question a. For those of you who love music, have you ever wanted a personal connection with your favorite artists? To know the real-life stories behind their music? 5. Put it all Together a. This site is the official site of DRM-Big-Music with a purpose and intent to connect listeners with the artist—in a more personal way—through the artist’s music and the stories behind them. In a world where a song is just a song, and an artist is just another artist, DRM-Big-Music has a goal to make music meaningful and relevant to all people and provide an easy way to find such content in a simple way, all in one place! ]

Audience

[The audience is for all music listeners! Especially for those of the younger generation (18-30) and those who enjoy listening to gospel and country-esc music.]

DRM Big Music--Home Page Info

[The Home Page for my website will be where listeners will come and find general information about the website. On this page there will be a navigation section at the top (that will be found on the other two pages as well) one button taking the listener to a music page and the about page. The middle button will be my logo with a link that takes the user back to the home page. The Home Page will also have a welcome letter/introduction video from the artist. At the bottom, general information can be found (links to music sites and socail media, copyright info, contact info, etc.)]
